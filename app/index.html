Note that...

  • you can load local files by dragging them onto the menu bar of the app,
  • your files and settings are stored only in your browser and are not uploaded anywhere,
  • video files must be in an HTML5-compatible format,
  • right-click on a Shadertoy lets you move the camera with the mouse,
  • ... and via W,A,S,D, Q,E, Z,C, toggle global Y-axis rotation pressing Y, and
  • ... change the stereo frustum via arrow and pagination keys.
  • For practical and legal reasons I only provide a few built-in examples.
